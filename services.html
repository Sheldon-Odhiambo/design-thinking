About Design Thinking Society

As Design Thinking Society, we guide and coach your internal cross-functional team by helping them identify and transform customer needs into novel business products, services, and models. We provide businesses with the following results:

  • We introduce the design thinking process to your organization.
  • We use the design thinking process to develop user-centered products and services.
  • We create innovation champions and equip them to help constantly realize business growth.

Trust us to guide your journey to innovation through DT – Most company leaders choose to innovate, but not all innovators take the streamlined design thinking process to success. As Design Thinking Society, we guide businesses through their innovation journey, resulting in consistent organic growth.

Book a meeting with us today, and we will help you revolutionize your business.

Who We Are

We are a human-centered design and innovation society. We can help you understand your customers and build products around their needs. Our role is to facilitate your process and skills using the design thinking process so that you can build your products and services with customer focus. With human-centered design, we will foster innovation strategies that grow your business and improve your bottom line.

Our Mission

To promote the use of design thinking as a problem-solving approach in Africa. We want to help business leaders in Africa adopt and execute the human-centered design mindset and process. We want to encourage pioneering leaders to foster organizational cultures that advocate for the betterment of their customers, employees, and society.

Our final goal is to turn team members into design thinking coaches capable of human-centered design. We believe that when you truly connect empathically with your customers – and design your products and services from their perspective – you will find solutions that customers will love.

Our Vision

Our vision is to coagulate an ecosystem of leaders, organizations and businesses who are working towards the same goal: designing experiences that delight millions of people, while also solving the most pressing challenges in our communities and our planet.
